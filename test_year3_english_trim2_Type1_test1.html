Listening Passage (Cyprus):

Cyprus is a great island that attracts families looking to stay in holiday villas or self catering holiday apartments. People love to spend their vacation at this travel destination with their family and friends in order to have a time of complete joy and excitement. The best part about Cyprus is that its tourist attractions are well located and distributed all around the beautiful island. All the nature lovers can conveniently enjoy the scenic and outstanding beauty of Cyprus by walking on the outstanding trails in the mountains. The first thing that the travelers will surely love in Cyprus is snowboarding and skiing. Cyprus is soon becoming the established destination for exciting winter sports. Skiing is a great way to spend your time in the beautiful natural landscapes of Cyprus along with enjoying your favorite activity. Watersports is another thing that can keep you busy while holidaying in Cyprus. Opportunities about this island for the lovers of activities like swimming, paragliding and windsurfing.

Listening Comprehension - Correction

1. Correction:

Correct answer:

b- recommend Cyprus as a perfect tourist destination.

Explanation: The text describes Cyprus positively, highlighting its attractions and activities without any complaints. The purpose is clearly to recommend it as a destination.

2. Correction:

Corrected statements:

1. "Cyprus attracts families looking to stay in holiday villas or self catering holiday apartments."

2. "Tourist attractions are well located and distributed all around the beautiful island."

Explanation: 1. The text mentions both villas AND apartments, not just apartments.
2. The text explicitly states attractions are "distributed all around" the island, not gathered in one place.

3. Correction:

Completed sentence:

All the nature lovers can conveniently enjoy the scenic and outstanding beauty of Cyprus by walking on the outstanding trails in the mountains.

Explanation: These words are directly from the listening text.

4. Correction:

Correct activities mentioned:

1. snowboarding
2. skiing
3. swimming
4. paragliding
5. windsurfing

Note: The text mentions 5 activities, but the question asks for "only four among the following". Any 4 of these 5 are acceptable.

5. Correction:

Words from phonetic transcriptions:

/ ski n/ = skiing

Explanation: These words appear in the listening text.

Language Section - Correction

1. Correction:

Completed text:

The Edinburgh Festival is hugely popular with tourists. It is actually an umbrella term for a group of independently organized festivals, which together bring you a spectacular variety of events to choose from. The Edinburgh International Book Festival offers talks, readings as well as discussions, musical events and numerous children's activities. Tickets need to be booked well in advance as accommodation isn't easy to find in Edinburgh during the festival. Edinburgh is a compact city with good public transport, so you can stay a few miles out and make the most of the Festival, whatever time of day or night you choose to experience it.

Explanation: The two unused words are "less" and "as".

2. Correction:

Correct word forms:

Upon entering Qatar, it will be impossible for the visitors to feel alienated. The generosity and hospitality of the Qataris can be felt everywhere. This atmosphere is a reflection of their strong bend to their homeland, their traditions and their culture, which has been passed down from century to century. 3. Correction:

Correct alternatives:

1. then
2. significant
3. for
4. disappointment
5. or
6. be
7. broken
8. unfair

Detailed Explanation:
1. "then" - adverb meaning "at that time"
2. "significant" - adjective modifying "changes"
3. "ask for" - phrasal verb meaning "request"
4. "disappointment" - noun form needed
5. "or" - conjunction showing alternative
6. "be" - infinitive form after "would"
7. "broken" - past participle in present perfect tense
8. "unfair" - adjective meaning "not fair"
