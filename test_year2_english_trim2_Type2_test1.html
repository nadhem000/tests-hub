III- Writing - Planning & sample article

Planning for argumentative article

  • Introduction: Present the topic (school uniforms) and state your thesis (e.g., I believe uniforms should be abolished / maintained).
  • Argument 1 (for/against): First point with explanation and example.
  • Argument 2: Second point with support.
  • Counter-argument (optional): Address opposing view and refute it.
  • Conclusion: Summarize and restate opinion.

Sample article (30 lines)

Title: Should School Uniforms Be Abolished?

School uniforms have been a topic of debate for decades. While some believe they promote equality and discipline, others argue they suppress individuality. In my opinion, uniforms should be abolished because they restrict personal expression and do not necessarily improve academic performance.

Firstly, uniforms stifle students' creativity and individuality. Adolescence is a time when young people explore their identity, and clothing is a key form of self-expression. Forcing everyone to dress the same can make students feel like they are part of a system rather than individuals. For example, many students feel more confident and motivated when they can wear clothes that reflect their personality.

Secondly, the argument that uniforms reduce bullying based on clothing is weak. Bullying often shifts to other aspects like accessories, shoes, or even mobile phones. Moreover, uniforms can be expensive for low-income families, especially when they have to buy specific branded items. This creates financial pressure rather than equality.

Opponents claim that uniforms create a sense of belonging and reduce distractions. However, a positive school environment depends more on teaching respect and tolerance than on what students wear. Schools that have abolished uniforms often report no decline in discipline.

In conclusion, while uniforms have some benefits, they are not essential for a good education. Allowing students to choose their own clothes fosters independence and prepares them for real-world decisions. Therefore, I strongly believe that school uniforms should be abolished.
