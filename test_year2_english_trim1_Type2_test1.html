II. Language - Correction

1. Correction:

Correct answers:

1. with (reconnect with something/someone)
2. Instead (instead of = in place of)
3. forget (forget X for Y = replace X with Y)
4. less (comparative form, less capable)
5. who (refers to people, subject pronoun)
6. huge (adjective meaning very big)

Detailed Explanation:
1. "with" - Correct preposition with "reconnect"
2. "Instead" - "Instead of" is the correct phrase meaning "in place of"
3. "forget" - Means to abandon or neglect in favor of something else
4. "less" - Comparative adjective needed (less capable than before)
5. "who" - Relative pronoun for people as subject
6. "huge" - Emphasizes the seriousness of the problem
