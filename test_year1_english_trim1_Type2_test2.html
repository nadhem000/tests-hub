Part III: Writing - Correction

Task 1: Correction:

Sample dialogue:

Mother: John, I noticed you've been coming home late from school these past few days.

John: I'm sorry, Mom. I've been staying late for football practice.

Mother: But you used to be home by 4 PM. What's keeping you so late?

John: Our coach extended practice time because we have an important match next week.

Mother: Are you sure it's just football? Your grades have been dropping too.

John: I know, Mom. I've been struggling with math, but I'm trying my best.

Mother: I'm worried about you. If your grades don't improve, I might have to restrict your football practice.

John: Please don't do that! Football is really important to me. I'll study more, I promise.

Mother: Okay, let's make a deal. You can continue football if you promise to study for at least two hours every day.

John: Thank you, Mom! I promise I'll study hard and improve my grades.

Note: A good dialogue should flow naturally, show realistic responses, and maintain consistent character voices.

Your loving son/daughter,
